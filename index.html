A little about myself...

Welcome to my portfolio!

I wish I could say web development has always been a passion of mine, however it was recently in the past year it really hit me. My "aha" moment struck while I was in Kenya due to a military obligation. Local Kenyan villagers had access to the internet, smartphones, and Facebook, nonetheless they still walked 3 miles to get water. Physical infrastructure took a lot of money and time to come to fruition, however those local citizens had access to what we all do in first world countries, an equal playing field to start an online business and generate commerce digitally. It hadn't occured to me for a long time that coding is a way to express and impact the world, one keystroke at a time. All this intuition has led me to attending Eleven Fifty Academy (2022 Web Development Grad) to hone my technical skill-set utilizing these technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, PostgreSQL, TypeScript, and Node.js. I haven’t looked back and have been building ever since… Ultimately I’m looking to make a positive impact at an organization that I can help develop the next revolution of future products and services.
